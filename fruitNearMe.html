I found lots of farmers markets with delicious fruit near you! The closest one is this one, it's called the Tougas Family Farm, located in Northborough, Massachusetts. I have not been here myself, but I am sure it is amazing!

Tougas Family Farm offers more than just super fun fruit picking for your whole family, but they also have a barnyard, playground and kitchen to spice up your time at the farm.
